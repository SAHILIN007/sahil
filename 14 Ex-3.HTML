Bugatti La Voiture Noire

La Voiture Noire is a tribute to the Type 57 SC Atlantic, a rare Bugatti model from the 1930s. Only four Atlantics were ever created, and La Voiture Noire celebrates the missing fourth Atlantic that disappeared during World War II. With its elongated silhouette, central fin, and six tailpipes, this hypercar blends speed, luxury, and automotive haute couture. Designed by Jean Bugatti, it sold for over $12 million, making it an unparalleled masterpiece in the world of automobiles.
